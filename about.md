# Player Advertisements
### <co>Entry for Modtober 2025: What The Heck!?</c>

<cj>You love advertisements, right?</c> Who doesn't?! Well, maybe you want to <cg>*promote some of your own levels*</c> with over-the-top, eye catching, MrBeast-type clickbaity advertisements! Or, maybe you just want to see some very funny ads while you play. Either way, <cy>this mod is for **everyone**</c>!

<cl>This mod adds advertisements for levels everywhere in the game!</c> In most areas of the game, you will likely find an advertisement for someone's level! You can also click on these advertisements to get sent to their totally awesome levels.

This mod is the perfect tool to find cool levels you otherwise may not have heard of! So, what are you waiting for? <cg>DOWNLOAD NOW!</c>

#### <cy>Want to submit YOUR OWN advertisement? Do so at the [Ads Manager](https://ads.arcticwoof.xyz)!</c>
